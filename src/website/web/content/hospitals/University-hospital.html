About Us

It was established according to the latest international models and is the largest in terms of modern facilities. It includes 43 beds, 13 operating rooms with the most modern capsule system in the world that prevents infection, 64 separate intensive care rooms with a complete isolation system, radiology devices that are considered among the best in international hospitals, a cardiology and catheterization department, and a special wing for the women and children department.
