About Batlim Resort

Baltim Summer Resort is an Egyptian city located in the Burullus District of Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate in Egypt.

The district's capital is Baltim City, located south of Baltim Summer Resort City. The city overlooks the Mediterranean Sea for 11 km.

It is the northernmost region in Egypt and is a summer resort on the Mediterranean coast. It is known as a family resort.
