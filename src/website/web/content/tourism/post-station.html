About King Fouad Post Station

For decades, eyes have been focused on the King Fouad Station in Kafr El Sheikh, filled with longing for a station that witnessed the arrival of King Fouad, along with Arab and foreign delegations and state leaders who would visit the station to head to King Fouad's palace. Not to mention the distinguished sight of the king's entourage each time the king arrived at his palace in Kafr El Sheikh.

Over the decades, the station has become a haven for rodents and outlaws. Youm7 conducted a number of investigations, and the response came on May 30, 2023, from Major General Gamal Nour El-Din, former Governor of Kafr El-Sheikh, to restore and maintain it. After completing the upgrade, development, and restoration work of the historic King Fouad Train Station, it was converted into a post office to provide all integrated governmental, postal, and financial services, as well as Egypt's digital services to citizens.

On December 16, 2023, Dr. Amr Talaat, Minister of Communications and Information Technology, and Lieutenant General Engineer Kamel El-Wazir, Minister of Transport, accompanied by Major General Gamal Nour El-Din, former Governor of Kafr El-Sheikh, inaugurated the "King Fouad Station" post office in the Manshiyat Fouad area of ​​Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate.
