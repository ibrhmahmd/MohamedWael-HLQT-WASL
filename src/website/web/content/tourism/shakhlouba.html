About Shakhlouba Island

Al-Shakhlouba Island is a charming and beautiful spot on the map of Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate, located in Lake Burullus, towards Sidi Salem Center. It is an old fish market in the middle of the lake. Because of its beauty and charm,

the fish market moved from there to the shore of the lake in Al-Shakhlouba village, making the island an important tourist attraction. Because of its beauty, it was called the Bride of Burullus and the Venice of Egypt. Al-Shakhlouba village is located between two drains, No. 9 and No. 8.

It was called Al-Shakhlouba because it was a large swamp before the construction of houses around it, and the Shakhlouba fish gathered there, so it was given this name.
